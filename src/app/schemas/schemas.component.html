Schema: inventorydb

The inventorydb (database) has 4 tables:

Items - this has 2 foreign keys to table ItemTypes and ItemLocations

ItemTypes - to describe what type/group an item may be

ItemLocations - to keep track of where the item may be

ItemImage - possibly an image of the item, this table has a foreign key to table Items
